<p><p>The dental burs market encompasses various shank types essential for different dental procedures. Long Straight Shank (HP) burs are designed for handpieces that require a longer reach, providing versatility in treatments. Latch-type Shank (RA) burs feature a latch mechanism for stability, commonly used in contra-angle handpieces for easy bur replacement. Friction Grip Shank (FG) burs are characterized by their compact design, ensuring a secure grip in high-speed handpieces, allowing for precise cutting and shaping in dental applications.</p></p>
