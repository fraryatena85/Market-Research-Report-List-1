<p><p>Fanfold corrugated packaging is categorized into single-walled and double-walled types. Single-walled fanfold consists of one layer of fluted cardboard, offering moderate strength and flexibility, suitable for lightweight products and shipping. Double-walled fanfold, on the other hand, features two layers of fluting, providing enhanced durability and protection for heavier items. This type is ideal for more demanding applications, such as industrial shipping and storage. Both types cater to various sectors, emphasizing efficiency and cost-effectiveness in packaging solutions.</p></p>
