<p><strong>The Concrete Pump Trucks Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Piston Mode</li><li>Squeeze Mode</li><li>Hydraulic Diaphragm Mode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Concrete pump trucks are classified into three main types based on their operation mechanism. Piston mode utilizes a piston to create pressure and move concrete through the pump, ensuring a consistent flow. Squeeze mode employs rollers that compress a tube to push concrete forward, offering flexibility for various site conditions. Hydraulic diaphragm mode uses a diaphragm to generate pressure through hydraulic fluid, allowing for precise control and reduced wear. Each type caters to specific construction needs and project requirements.</p></p>
