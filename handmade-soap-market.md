<p><strong>The Handmade Soap Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CP (Cold Process)</li><li>HP (Hot Process)</li><li>MP (Melt & Pour)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The handmade soap market features three primary types: Cold Process (CP), Hot Process (HP), and Melt & Pour (MP). CP involves mixing oils and lye at room temperature, allowing for intricate designs but requiring curing time. HP involves cooking the soap mixture, which accelerates the saponification process, yielding a finished product more quickly. MP involves melting pre-made soap base and adding custom ingredients, making it user-friendly for beginners. Each method has unique attributes catering to different consumer preferences.</p></p>
