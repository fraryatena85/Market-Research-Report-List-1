<p><ul><li>Closed Cell</li><li>Open Cell</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Gray Foam Glass market is divided into two main types: closed cell and open cell. Closed cell foam glass consists of tightly packed glass bubbles, providing high compressive strength, moisture resistance, and thermal insulation, making it ideal for protective and structural applications. Conversely, open cell foam glass has interconnected cells, offering enhanced sound absorption and lightweight properties, suitable for applications in acoustics and lightweight insulation. Both types cater to different industrial needs, emphasizing thermal and sound insulation capabilities.</p></p>
