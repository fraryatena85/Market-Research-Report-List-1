<p><ul><li>Liquid Biofertilizers</li><li>Carrier-based Biofertilizers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The seed treatment biofertilizers market comprises two main types: liquid biofertilizers and carrier-based biofertilizers. Liquid biofertilizers are easy to apply and can effectively deliver beneficial microorganisms directly to seeds or soil, promoting healthy plant growth. Carrier-based biofertilizers, on the other hand, involve the use of a solid substrate to hold and deliver these microorganisms, allowing for longer shelf life and easier handling. Both types enhance nutrient availability, improve soil health, and support sustainable agriculture practices.</p></p>
