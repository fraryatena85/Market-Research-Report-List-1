<p><p>The organic soybean by-products market encompasses three primary categories: soybean oil, oilcake, and lecithin. Soybean oil is a versatile cooking and industrial oil known for its health benefits and use in food products. Oilcake, the remaining material after oil extraction, serves as a high-protein animal feed ingredient. Lecithin, a natural emulsifier derived from soybeans, is utilized in food processing and pharmaceuticals to improve texture and stability. Together, these by-products enhance sustainability and value in the organic agriculture sector.</p></p>
