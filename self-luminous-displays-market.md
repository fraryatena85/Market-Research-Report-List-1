<p><p>The self-luminous displays market comprises various technologies, primarily OLED (Organic Light Emitting Diodes) and QLED (Quantum Dot Light Emitting Diodes). OLED displays feature individual pixel emission, resulting in vibrant colors and deep blacks, making them ideal for high-quality visuals. QLED, while reliant on a backlight, enhances color accuracy and brightness through quantum dot technology. Other emerging technologies may include MicroLED and AMOLED, which aim to combine the advantages of OLED and QLED, catering to diverse consumer preferences and applications in the display industry.</p></p>
