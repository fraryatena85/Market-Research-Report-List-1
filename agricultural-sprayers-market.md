<p><p>The agricultural sprayers market is categorized into three main types: ultra-low volume, low volume, and high volume. Ultra-low volume sprayers use minimal liquid but deliver effective coverage, often utilizing concentrated solutions for targeted applications. Low volume sprayers apply a moderate amount of liquid, suitable for smaller areas or specific crops. High volume sprayers utilize larger quantities of liquid for expansive coverage, ideal for broadacre farming. Each type serves unique agricultural needs based on crop type, pest control, and application efficiency.</p></p>
