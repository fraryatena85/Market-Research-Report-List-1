Colposcopy tests follow-up by providing a detailed examination of the cervix using magnification, allowing for targeted biopsies if abnormalities are found. The Endocervical Curettage (ECC) procedure is used to sample cells from the cervical canal, aiding in the diagnosis of invasive cells. Together, these methods enhance early detection and treatment strategies.</p></p>
