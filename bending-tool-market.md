<p><p>The bending tool market is categorized into three main types: manual, electric, and hydraulic. Manual bending tools rely on physical effort, making them cost-effective and portable, suitable for small-scale projects. Electric bending tools provide enhanced efficiency and precision, ideal for medium-scale tasks, often featuring automated mechanisms. Hydraulic bending tools utilize fluid pressure to achieve significant bending force, perfect for heavy-duty applications and large-scale manufacturing. Each type serves different needs based on application, capacity, and user preference.</p></p>
