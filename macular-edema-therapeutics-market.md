<p><p>The Macular Edema therapeutics market is categorized into several treatment types aimed at reducing fluid accumulation in the macula. Laser treatment uses focused light to seal leaky blood vessels, thereby decreasing swelling. Anti-VEGF injections inhibit vascular endothelial growth factor to prevent abnormal blood vessel growth. Anti-inflammatory treatments reduce inflammation and edema through medications like corticosteroids. Vitrectomy involves surgical removal of the vitreous gel to alleviate traction and address any underlying causes, promoting better retinal health.</p></p>
