<p><p>The Polyethylene Lidding Films market comprises three main types: Single Layer Films, Dual Layer Films, and Multilayer Films. Single Layer Films are made from a single polyethylene layer, offering cost-effectiveness and simplicity. Dual Layer Films combine two layers for enhanced barrier properties, improving shelf life and product protection. Multilayer Films consist of multiple layers, often incorporating various materials, delivering superior strength and customization options for specific applications, making them ideal for complex packaging needs. Each type caters to different market requirements and performance standards.</p></p>
