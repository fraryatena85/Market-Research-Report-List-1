<p><ul><li>Wafer Type</li><li>Lug Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high-performance butterfly valves market is primarily divided into two types: wafer type and lug type. Wafer type valves are designed for installation between flanges, providing a compact and lightweight solution ideal for space-constrained applications. They feature a simple design, enabling quick assembly and disassembly. Conversely, lug type valves have threaded lugs that allow for easy mounting and removal without disturbing the pipeline, making them suitable for more challenging scenarios where maintenance is required without system downtime.</p></p>
