<p><p>The Non-Agriculture Smart Irrigation System market comprises two main types: weather-based controllers and sensor-based controllers. Weather-based controllers utilize real-time weather data to optimize irrigation schedules, ensuring efficient water use by adjusting based on rainfall and temperature conditions. In contrast, sensor-based controllers rely on soil moisture and environmental sensors to determine the exact irrigation needs of plants. Both types aim to enhance water conservation, reduce operational costs, and promote sustainable practices in non-agricultural settings like landscaping and parks.</p></p>
