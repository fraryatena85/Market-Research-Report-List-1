<p><p>The Flame Retardant PC/ABS Plastic market is primarily segmented into two types based on processing methods: Injection Molding and Extrusion Molding. Injection Molding involves injecting molten plastic into a mold, allowing for complex shapes and high-volume production, making it suitable for components like housings and automotive parts. Extrusion Molding, on the other hand, involves forcing the plastic through a die to create continuous shapes, such as sheets and profiles, ideal for applications in construction and electrical enclosures.</p></p>
