<p><ul><li>Single Front</li><li>Double Front</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Low Voltage Switchboards market is categorized into two main types: Single Front and Double Front switchboards. Single Front switchboards have all components accessible from one side, making them space-efficient and suitable for locations with limited access. In contrast, Double Front switchboards allow access from both sides, facilitating maintenance and operation while providing redundancy. Both types are essential for distributing electrical power safely and efficiently in commercial and industrial settings, with selection depending on space availability and operational requirements.</p></p>
