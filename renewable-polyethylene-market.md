<p><p>The renewable polyethylene market comprises three main types: HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene), LLDPE (Linear Low-Density Polyethylene), and LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene). HDPE is known for its strength and durability, making it suitable for rigid containers and piping. LLDPE features enhanced flexibility and impact resistance, often used in films and packaging. LDPE is characterized by its softness and low-density structure, commonly found in bags and wraps. Each type plays a vital role in various applications, promoting sustainable practices in plastic production.</p></p>
