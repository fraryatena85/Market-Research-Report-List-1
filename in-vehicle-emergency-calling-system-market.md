Automatic eCall systems detect severe accidents through vehicle sensors and automatically alert emergency services, providing location and crash data. In contrast, Manual Button eCall relies on the driver or passengers to initiate the call by pressing a button in emergencies. Both types enhance road safety, ensuring prompt emergency response, but cater to different user interactions during critical situations.</p></p>
