<p><p>Cat litter trappers come in two main market types: single layer and double layer. Single layer trappers consist of a single mesh or material designed to capture litter, making them easy to clean but potentially less effective at preventing scattered debris. Double layer trappers feature two layers of material, enhancing litter retention by allowing granules to fall through the top layer while retaining them in the bottom layer, offering improved functionality and cleanliness, appealing to pet owners seeking efficient solutions.</p></p>
