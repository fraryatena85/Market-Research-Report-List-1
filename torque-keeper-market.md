<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Torque Keeper Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Friction Type</li><li>Shear Pin Type</li><li>Roller Release Detent Type</li><li>Magnetic Torque Limiter Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Torque Keeper market comprises various types designed to protect machinery from overload. Friction Type Torque Keepers use friction to withstand torque until it exceeds a set limit, triggering a slip. Shear Pin Type relies on a designed pin that breaks under excessive torque, preventing further damage. Roller Release Detent Type features rollers that disengage when torque exceeds the predetermined level, allowing safe operation. Finally, Magnetic Torque Limiter Type employs magnetic fields to disengage at specific torque thresholds, ensuring equipment safety and efficiency.</p></p>
