<p><p>Orthokeratology lenses, also known as ortho-k lenses, are specially designed gas-permeable contact lenses worn overnight to reshape the cornea temporarily. As awareness of myopia management grows and the prevalence of eye conditions increases, the demand for orthokeratology lenses is on the rise.</p><p>The Orthokeratology lens market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.00% during the forecast period.
