<p><p>BGA test probes are essential tools in semiconductor testing, differentiating primarily into short stroke and long stroke types. Short stroke test probes, typically used for applications requiring minimal movement, offer higher precision for tightly packed connections. In contrast, long stroke test probes accommodate greater displacement, facilitating connections in more versatile setups or larger component arrays. Both types are crucial for ensuring the reliability and performance of BGA (Ball Grid Array) packages, impacting the overall efficiency of electronic manufacturing and testing processes.</p></p>
