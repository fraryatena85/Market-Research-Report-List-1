<p><strong>The Terahertz Spectroscopy System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Terahertz Time Domain Spectroscopy</li><li>Terahertz Frequency Domain Spectroscopy</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Terahertz Spectroscopy System market is primarily categorized into two types: Terahertz Time Domain Spectroscopy (THz-TDS) and Terahertz Frequency Domain Spectroscopy (THz-FDS). THz-TDS utilizes short pulses of terahertz radiation to analyze materials' time-dependent properties, providing detailed information on molecular vibrations and interactions. In contrast, THz-FDS employs continuous-wave terahertz signals to assess frequency-specific characteristics, enabling precise measurements of absorption and refractive indices. Both methodologies are crucial in applications ranging from material science to biomedical research, enhancing analytical capabilities.</p></p>
