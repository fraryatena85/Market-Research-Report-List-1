<p><p>Absorption chillers are energy-efficient cooling systems that utilize heat to produce cold, distinguishing themselves in the market by single-stage and double-stage types. Single-stage chillers operate on a single cycle, making them simpler and ideal for moderate cooling applications. In contrast, double-stage chillers involve two cycles to achieve lower temperatures and greater efficiency, suitable for more demanding cooling requirements. The choice between these types depends on factors like application needs, energy sources, and desired efficiency levels.</p></p>
