<p><p>The refrigerated warehouse market can be categorized primarily into two main types: public and private. Public refrigerated warehouses provide storage services to multiple clients, offering flexibility and scalability for businesses that need temperature-controlled environments without the commitment of owning a facility. In contrast, private refrigerated warehouses are owned and operated by a single company, providing tailored storage solutions that meet specific business needs. This type allows for greater control over operations and inventory management but requires a larger upfront investment.</p></p>
