<p><p>Blockchain supply chain markets are categorized into three types: Public, Private, and Consortium Blockchains. </p><p>Public Blockchains allow anyone to participate and view transactions, promoting transparency and decentralization. Private Blockchains are restricted to specific participants, offering enhanced privacy and control, making them suitable for businesses needing confidentiality. Consortium Blockchains combine elements of both, enabling a group of organizations to share a blockchain network, facilitating collaboration while maintaining some level of privacy and governance among trusted participants.</p></p>
