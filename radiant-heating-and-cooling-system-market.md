<p><strong>The Radiant Heating And Cooling System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Embedded Surface Systems</li><li>Thermally Active Building Systems</li><li>Capillary Surface Systems</li><li>Radiant Panels</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The radiant heating and cooling system market encompasses several types, each designed to enhance energy efficiency and comfort in buildings. Embedded surface systems integrate heating or cooling elements into floors or ceilings, providing uniform temperature distribution. Thermally active building systems utilize building mass to store and redistribute thermal energy, optimizing climate control. Capillary surface systems feature small pipes or capillaries embedded in surfaces for precise temperature regulation. Radiant panels, typically mounted on walls or ceilings, deliver direct heating or cooling, promoting an efficient indoor environment.</p></p>
