<p><strong>The Handheld Ultrasonic Flow Meter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Clamp</li><li>Clip-on</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The handheld ultrasonic flow meter market is primarily categorized into clamp-on and clip-on types. Clamp-on meters utilize transducers that attach to the outer surface of pipes, allowing for non-invasive flow measurement of liquid or gas without needing to cut into the pipes. Clip-on meters are a more portable variant, designed for quick and easy installation, typically featuring a lightweight design for flexible use across different locations. Both types provide accurate flow readings without disrupting the fluid system.</p></p>
