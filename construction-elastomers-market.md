<p><ul><li>Thermoplastic</li><li>Thermoset Plastic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In the construction elastomers market, two primary types are thermoplastic elastomers (TPE) and thermoset elastomers. TPEs are flexible and can be repeatedly processed and reshaped when heated, making them suitable for various applications like sealants and adhesives. They offer excellent durability and elasticity. In contrast, thermoset elastomers are hardened through a curing process, providing superior thermal stability and chemical resistance. These are commonly used in applications requiring long-lasting performance, such as gaskets and flooring materials.</p></p>
