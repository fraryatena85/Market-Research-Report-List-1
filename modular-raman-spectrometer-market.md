<p><p>The Modular Raman Spectrometer market comprises several types, including Direct Coupling and Fiber Optic configurations. Direct Coupling Modular Raman Spectrometers are integrated systems that connect directly to the sample, providing high-resolution spectra and real-time analysis. In contrast, Fiber Optic Modular Raman Spectrometers use fiber optic cables to facilitate remote sampling, allowing analysis of hard-to-reach or hazardous materials. Both types are designed for flexibility and versatility in various applications, from pharmaceuticals to environmental monitoring, catering to diverse analytical needs.</p></p>
