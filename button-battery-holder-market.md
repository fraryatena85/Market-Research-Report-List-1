<p><p>The button battery holder market can be categorized into three main types: Surface Mount, Through Hole, and Others. Surface Mount holders are designed for placement directly onto the surface of a PCB, allowing compact designs and automated manufacturing. Through Hole holders feature leads that pass through the PCB, providing robust connections, typically used in more durable applications. The "Others" category encompasses specialized holders tailored for unique requirements or hybrid designs that combine features of both mounting types, catering to diverse electronic applications.</p></p>
