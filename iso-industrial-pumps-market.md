<p><strong>The ISO Industrial Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Centrifugal Pumps</li><li>Positive Displacement Pumps</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ISO industrial pumps market primarily comprises three types: centrifugal pumps, positive displacement pumps, and other pumps. Centrifugal pumps utilize rotational energy to move fluids, making them ideal for high flow rates and lower viscosity liquids. Positive displacement pumps operate by trapping a fixed amount of fluid and forcing it through the system, suitable for high-viscosity materials. The "other" category includes specialized pumps like peristaltic, diaphragm, and submersible pumps, catering to specific industrial applications and fluid characteristics.</p></p>
