<p><p>The Snacks Frying Machine market is primarily segmented into two types: batch and continuous. Batch frying machines process specific quantities of snacks at a time, allowing for flexibility and control over cooking times and flavors. They are ideal for small to medium-scale production. In contrast, continuous frying machines operate in a steady flow, cooking snacks continuously, which increases efficiency and output for large-scale production. Both types cater to different operational needs within the snacks manufacturing industry.</p></p>
