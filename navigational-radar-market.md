<p><p>The navigational radar market primarily includes two types: X Band and S Band radar systems. X Band radar operates at higher frequencies, offering superior resolution and enhanced detection capabilities, making it ideal for small vessels and coastal navigation. In contrast, S Band radar functions at lower frequencies, providing excellent range and weather penetration, suitable for larger vessels and long-distance tracking. Both types are crucial in maritime navigation, enhancing safety and operational efficiency in diverse marine environments.</p></p>
