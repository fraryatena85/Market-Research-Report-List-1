<p><ul><li>Microprismatic</li><li>Glass Beads</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The retro-reflective material market consists of two primary types: microprismatic and glass beads. Microprismatic materials utilize a prism-based design that reflects light efficiently, providing high visibility and durability for various applications, including traffic signage and safety apparel. In contrast, glass bead materials use small glass spheres to reflect light, offering a cost-effective solution for applications like road markings and vehicle graphics. Both types enhance safety by improving visibility in low-light conditions, each catering to specific performance needs and market demands.</p></p>
