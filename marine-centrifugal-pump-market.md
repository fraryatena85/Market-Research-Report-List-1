<p><strong>The Marine Centrifugal Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Stage Pump</li><li>Two-stage Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Marine Centrifugal Pump market primarily comprises single-stage and two-stage pumps, each designed for specific applications in marine environments. Single-stage pumps feature one impeller to deliver moderate flow rates and are ideal for tasks requiring lower pressure and simplicity. In contrast, two-stage pumps utilize two impellers, providing higher pressure and flow capacity, suitable for demanding applications such as deep-sea pumping and high-head requirements. Both types are essential for efficient fluid transfer in maritime operations.</p></p>
