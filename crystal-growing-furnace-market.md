<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Crystal Growing Furnace Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Czochralski (CZ) Method Furnace</li><li>Floating Zone (FZ) Method Furnace</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The crystal growing furnace market primarily includes two types: the Czochralski (CZ) method furnace and the Floating Zone (FZ) method furnace. The CZ method furnace is widely used for growing single crystals, particularly silicon, by pulling a seed crystal from a molten material, allowing for large-scale production. In contrast, the FZ method furnace allows for the growth of high-purity crystals by heating a rod in a localized zone, promoting uniformity and minimal contamination. Both methods serve critical roles in semiconductor and material science applications.</p></p>
