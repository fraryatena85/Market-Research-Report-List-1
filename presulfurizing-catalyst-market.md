<p><p>The presulfurizing catalyst market is categorized into two main types based on catalytic activity: Non-Catalytic Activity and Weak Catalytic Activity. Non-Catalytic Activity refers to processes that do not involve any significant catalytic reactions, relying instead on physical methods to achieve presulfurization. On the other hand, Weak Catalytic Activity signifies catalysts that exhibit minimal catalytic efficiency, facilitating reactions but not to the full extent of more potent catalysts. Both types play roles in altering the sulfur content in materials during processing.</p></p>
