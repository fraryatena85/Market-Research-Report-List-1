<p><p>The Light Detection and Ranging (LiDAR) market comprises several types based on application and technology deployment. Airborne LiDAR involves mounted systems on aircraft for large-scale topographic surveys. Terrestrial LiDAR utilizes ground-based systems for detailed capture of stationary objects and landscapes. Mobile LiDAR employs vehicle-mounted systems for real-time data collection while in motion, ideal for road and infrastructure projects. Short Range LiDAR focuses on close-proximity measurements, commonly used in industrial and robotics applications for precision tasks.</p></p>
