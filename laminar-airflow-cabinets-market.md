<p><strong>The Laminar Airflow Cabinets Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vertical Laminar Airflow Cabinets</li><li>Horizontal Laminar Airflow Cabinets</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The laminar airflow cabinets market is divided into two main types: vertical laminar airflow cabinets and horizontal laminar airflow cabinets. Vertical cabinets have airflow directed from the top down, providing a clean environment ideal for larger equipment or materials. In contrast, horizontal cabinets push air from the back towards the front, facilitating easier access for users and enhancing visibility. Both types are crucial in laboratories and cleanrooms, ensuring contamination control and maintaining the integrity of sensitive samples and processes.</p></p>
