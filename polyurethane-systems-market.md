<p><strong>The Polyurethane Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermoplastic Type</li><li>Thermosetting Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polyurethane systems market is divided into two main types: thermoplastic and thermosetting. Thermoplastic polyurethane (TPU) is characterized by its flexibility and ability to be reprocessed upon heating, making it ideal for applications requiring durability and impact resistance, such as in automotive and footwear industries. In contrast, thermosetting polyurethane remains rigid after curing and is known for its superior heat resistance and mechanical properties, making it suitable for applications in coatings, adhesives, and foams.</p></p>
