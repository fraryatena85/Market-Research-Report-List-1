<p><ul><li>Hot Chamber Die Casting Machine</li><li>Cold Chamber Die Casting Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Metal Die Casting Machinery market is primarily divided into two types: hot chamber and cold chamber die casting machines. Hot chamber die casting machines are suited for metals with low melting points, allowing for faster production cycles due to their integrated melting and injection systems. Conversely, cold chamber die casting machines are used for higher melting point metals, requiring separate melting furnaces. Each type serves specific industry needs, influencing the selection based on material properties and production requirements.</p></p>
