<p><ul><li>Mechanical</li><li>Hydraulic Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Plate rolls are categorized into two main market types: mechanical and hydraulic. Mechanical plate rolls utilize a system of gears and levers to control the bending process, offering durability and straightforward operation, suitable for lighter materials. In contrast, hydraulic plate rolls use hydraulic cylinders for bending, providing greater precision and the ability to handle thicker materials with ease. Each type is chosen based on specific project requirements, including material thickness, desired curvature, and production volume.</p></p>
