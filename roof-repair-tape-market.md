<p><p>The roof repair tape market comprises three main types: Bituminous tape, Butyl rubber tape, and Liquid silicone tape. Bituminous tape is ideal for sealing roofs and protecting against moisture, making it suitable for asphalt surfaces. Butyl rubber tape offers strong adhesion and flexibility, effectively sealing leaks in various roofing materials. Liquid silicone tape provides a waterproof, durable solution that cures to form a flexible layer, making it useful for both repairs and preventative maintenance on diverse roofs.</p></p>
