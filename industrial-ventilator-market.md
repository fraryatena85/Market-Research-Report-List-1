<p><p>The industrial ventilator market comprises three main types: inhalation, exclusion, and combined systems. Inhalation ventilators supply fresh air directly to workers, enhancing respiratory safety in confined or hazardous spaces. Exclusion ventilators focus on removing contaminated air and pollutants, ensuring a clean work environment. Combined systems integrate both functions, offering a versatile solution that enhances air quality and worker safety simultaneously. These types cater to various industrial needs, promoting healthier workspaces and compliance with safety regulations.</p></p>
