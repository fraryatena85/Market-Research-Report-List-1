<p><p>The Optical Projection Screen market features various types, including Fresnel and Black Grid screens. Fresnel screens utilize a series of ridged patterns to enhance light transmission and provide improved brightness, making them suitable for large displays in educational or commercial settings. Black Grid screens, on the other hand, incorporate a grid structure to reduce ambient light interference and enhance contrast, ideal for home theater systems. Both screen types are crucial for delivering high-quality visual experiences in diverse environments.</p></p>
