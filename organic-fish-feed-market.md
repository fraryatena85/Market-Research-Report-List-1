<p><p>The organic fish feed market is primarily segmented into two categories based on fish dietary needs: carnivorous and omnivorous fish. Carnivorous fish require high-protein feeds predominantly derived from fish meal, insects, and marine proteins to support their growth and health. In contrast, omnivorous fish thrive on a varied diet that includes both plants and animal sources, allowing for the incorporation of plant-based ingredients alongside protein-rich options. Each segment emphasizes sustainable sourcing and organic certification to meet consumer demand for eco-friendly aquaculture.</p></p>
