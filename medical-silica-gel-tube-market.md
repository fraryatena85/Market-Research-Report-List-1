<p><strong>The Medical Silica Gel Tube Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Extrusion Method</li><li>Mold Vulcanization</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Medical Silica Gel Tube market is primarily categorized into two types based on manufacturing processes: extrusion method and mold vulcanization. The extrusion method involves forcing silica gel materials through a die to create continuous tubes, offering uniformity and efficiency. In contrast, mold vulcanization involves shaping the silica gel in molds and subjecting it to heat and pressure, ensuring precise dimensions and enhanced durability. Both methods cater to diverse applications in the medical field, emphasizing quality and performance.</p></p>
