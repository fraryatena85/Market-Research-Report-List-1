<p><strong>The Single Zone VAV System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Duct</li><li>Dual-Duct</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Single Zone Variable Air Volume (VAV) system market includes Single-Duct and Dual-Duct systems. Single-Duct VAV systems supply conditioned air through a single duct, adjusting airflow to maintain desired temperature efficiently. Dual-Duct VAV systems, in contrast, utilize two separate ducts—one for heated and another for cooled air—mixing them at the terminal to achieve the desired temperature. Both systems enhance energy efficiency and comfort but cater to different building requirements, with Single-Duct being simpler and Dual-Duct offering more flexibility.</p></p>
