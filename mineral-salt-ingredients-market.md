<p><p>The mineral salt ingredients market is segmented into three main types: macro mineral salts, micro mineral salts, and trace elements. Macro mineral salts, such as calcium and magnesium, are required in larger quantities for essential bodily functions. Micro mineral salts, including zinc and iron, are needed in smaller amounts but are crucial for various biochemical processes. Trace elements, like selenium and iodine, are vital in minuscule quantities, playing pivotal roles in metabolism and overall health maintenance.</p></p>
