<p><p>The Low Noise Blue-Violet Laser market is segmented into two primary types: Single Mode and Multi-Mode. Single Mode lasers emit light through a single spatial mode, offering high precision and low noise, making them ideal for applications requiring tight beam quality, such as telecommunications and high-resolution imaging. In contrast, Multi-Mode lasers allow multiple light modes to propagate, providing broader output and higher power levels, suitable for applications like industrial laser cutting and medical therapies where beam intensity is crucial.</p></p>
